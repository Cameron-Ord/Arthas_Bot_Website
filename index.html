What is Arthas bot?

Arthas Bot is a discord bot programmed in JavaScript. It's purpose is to serve as a utility resource for World of Warcraft.

How does it work?

At the moment the bot is still a WIP. However, it's main function is serving as a queriable bot that fetches information from the WoWHead wiki.
